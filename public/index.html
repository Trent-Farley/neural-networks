Introduction

Neural networks will be the future of every industry. The amount of data for humans to process as the world advances is staggering, and with the help of neural networks, the copious amounts of information can be processed easier. There are multiple types of networks that can be made, with all of them being better at doing a task. From self-driving cars to finding your friend's photo that they just posted, it is all using neural networks, or also known as machine learning.

So what is a neural network?

To put it simply, neural network is a way of processing information to become summarized into something that is understandable. A network is how a machine learns, which is a combination of evolution and trial and error. To start one, the programmer creates a solution and if the smaller parts or "Nodes".
